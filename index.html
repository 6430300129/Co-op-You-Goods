Why You-Goods?

You-Goods is a well-established manufacturer of custom promotional and resale products such as acrylic keychains, rubber keychains, acrylic phone stands, custom microfiber products, and more. We have been operating out of Japan and Thailand for over 15 years and have helped thousands of satisfied customers create their custom keychains, microfiber products, and more.

In Japan we have a saying, 'おもてなし’ (omotenashi), which is an embodiment of Japanese culture, and the Japanese mindset. The phrase comes from the traditions of tea ceremonies, and it means to look after guests and customers with your entire heart and soul without expectation of reciprocity. Omotenashi can be seen throughout all sectors of Japanese service, and we want to extend this to our customers as well. Experience Japan through the commitment we here at You-Goods have made to bringing the spirit of omotenashi to every single person who comes across us.
